Suṇātu me bhante / āvuso saṅgho,9 ajj'uposatho paṇṇaraso / cātuddaso / sāmaggo,10 yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṁ, saṅgho uposathaṁ kareyya pātimokkhaṁ11 uddiseyya.12

Kiṁ saṅghassa pubbakiccaṁ?

Pārisuddhiṁ āyasmanto ārocetha. Pātimokkhaṁ13 uddisissāmi. Taṁ sabb'eva santā sādhukaṁ suṇoma manasikaroma. Yassa siyā āpatti, so āvikareyya.14 Asantiyā āpattiyā, tuṇhī bhavitabbaṁ. Tuṇhībhāvena kho pan'āyasmante parisuddhā'ti vedissāmi.

Yathā kho pana paccekapuṭṭhassa veyyākaraṇaṁ hoti, evam'evaṁ15 evarūpāya parisāya yāvatatiyaṁ anussāvitaṁ16 hoti.

Yo pana bhikkhu yāvatatiyaṁ anussāviyamāne17 saramāno santiṁ āpattiṁ n'āvikareyya,18 sampajānamusāvād'assa hoti. Sampajānamusāvādo kho pan'āyasmanto antarāyiko dhammo vutto bhagavatā. Tasmā saramānena bhikkhunā āpannena19 visuddh'āpekkhena20 santī āpatti21 āvikātabbā,22 āvikatā23 hi'ssa phāsu hoti.24
