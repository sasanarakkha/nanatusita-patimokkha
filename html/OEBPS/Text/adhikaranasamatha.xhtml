Adhikaranasamatha

Ime kho pan'āyasmanto satta adhikaraṇasamathā486 dhammā uddesaṁ āgacchanti.

Uppann'uppannānaṁ adhikaraṇānaṁ samathāya vūpasamāya: sammukhāvinayo dātabbo, sativinayo dātabbo, amūḷhavinayo487 dātabbo, paṭiññāya kāretabbo,488 yebhuyyasikā,489 tassapāpiyyasikā,490 tiṇ'avatthārako'ti.

Uddiṭṭhā kho āyasmanto satta adhikaraṇasamathā491 dhammā. Tatth'āyasmante pucchāmi: Kacci'ttha parisuddhā?
Dutiyam'pi pucchāmi: Kacci'ttha parisuddhā?
Tatiyam'pi pucchāmi: Kacci'ttha parisuddhā?
Parisuddh'etth'āyasmanto, tasmā tuṇhī, evam'etaṁ dhārayāmi.492
